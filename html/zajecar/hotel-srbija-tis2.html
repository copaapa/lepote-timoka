Hotel Srbija Tis

Hotel Srbija Tis in Zajecar is located in the heart of the city, next to the town square. It offers a large selection of single, double and triple rooms and luxury apartments. The hotel restaurant offers its guests a large selection of dishes from traditional local cuisine, and these dishes are prepared by top chefs. The hotel also has a conference room, where various presentations, anniversaries or business meetings can be held with a capacity of up to 400 guests. It consists of a restaurant, a ceremonial hall, an aperitif bar, a blue lounge, a banquet hall and a terrace. As we have already mentioned, there are single, double, triple rooms and lux suites. All rooms are equipped with all accompanying facilities, such as free WiFi, safe, mini bar, cable TV, bathroom with shower and air conditioning. In addition to excellent accommodation, the hotel also has a parking lot with video surveillance, as well as a sauna, gym, exchange office, table tennis room, casino and mini-market. The hotel is located on Nikola Pasic bb.
