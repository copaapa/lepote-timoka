Days of Hajduk Veljko

Hajduk Veljko Days are held in the second half of July, in the village of Lenovac, the birthplace of Hajduk Veljko, 15 km from Zajecar. It is a cultural and sports event, which aims to preserve from oblivion memory of the great Serbian hero from the First Serbian Uprising - hajduk Veljko Petrovic.

The program begins with the ceremonial laying of a wreath at the monument to Hajduk Veljko in Zajecar at 8 am, followed by Hierarch's liturgy in the Church of St. Nicholas in Lenovac.

After that, at around 10 am, a wreath will be laid at the memorial plaque to Hajduk Veljko in Lenovac, and sports competitions such as Hajdučki harambaša are held until 7 pm, as well as in tug of war, and finally there are indoor soccer games.

After the end of the music and stage program in the evening, the event ends with the selection for Hajduk's Beauty.

The organizers of this event are the Center for Culture of the city of Zajecar and the local community of Lenovac and the patron is the city of Zajecar itself.
