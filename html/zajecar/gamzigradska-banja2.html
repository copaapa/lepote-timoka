Gamzigradska spa

Gamzigradska spa is 11 km away from Zajecar, and 220 km from Belgrade. It lies at 160 meters above sea level, on the very shores of Black Timok. This spa is influenced by the continental climate, is surrounded by forests, and represents great place for treatment, rest and recreation. It consists of five springs, and the water temperature is between 37-40 degrees Celsius.

Although the springs of Gamzigrad Spa have been used for treatment before, only after the Second World War, more precisely, in 1954, a decision was made on the arrangement and protection of the spa. In the sixties of the last century, a hotel was built Castrum for accommodation of patients and other guests.

In this spa, specialized rehabilitation of peripheral blood vessel diseases is performed, such as disorders of the arterial blood vessels and lymphatic system. General rehabilitation is also performed, if someone has problems with connective tissue diseases, articular and extraarticular forms of rheumatism, neurological diseases and gynecological disorders. Several types of therapeutic baths and various modern devices where the hyperbaric chamber stands out, with overall medical help, it gives positive results.

Today, Gamzigradska spa has about 1000 beds for its guests, with all private houses offering overnight services.
