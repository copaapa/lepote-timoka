Days of Zoran Radmilovic

The manifestation "Days of Zoran Radmilović" has been held every autumn in Zaječar since 1993 in the National Theater of the Timok Region "Zoran Radmilović", under the auspices of the "Zoran Radmilović" Foundation, the Ministry of Culture and the City of Zaječar. The most famous theaters from the country and the region take part in it.

This event is named after the most famous Zajecar man and actor, Zoran Radmilović. His most famous theatrical productions are "King Ibi" and "Radovan III".

    The list of prizes to be awarded is:
  • Best Acting Award;
  • Visual Identity Award;
  • Best Performance Award;
  • Special Jury Award;
  • Audience Award for Best Performance.

    • The award for the best acting achievement, more precisely "Zoran's mustache", has so far been given to many famous and respected actors of the Serbian theater. such as: Anica Dobra, Milan Lane Gutović, Lazar Ristovski, Žarko Laušević, Dragan Mićanović, Sergej Trifunović, Dragan Mićanović, Nebojša Dugalić, Nenad Jezdić, Irfan Mensur, Petar Kralj, Miodrag Pejaković and many others.
