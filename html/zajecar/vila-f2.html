Villa F

Villa F is located at Dubrovačka 8 in Zaječar. This villa was opened in 2013 and consists of thirteen rooms, and consists of single, double rooms and suites. All accommodation units comprise cable TV, a work desk, a bedside table, a telephone, internet, a bathroom with a shower and air conditioning. The apartments have two separate rooms, one for dining and relaxing, and a separate bedroom with terrace. The restaurant of this villa has a capacity of up to 50 guests, and offers specialties of local and Italian cuisine. It is located on the reception level, and is open to both hotel guests and guests staying there.
