Villa Tamaris

Villa Tamaris is located in the center of Zajecar in the street Ljube Nešića 58, and consists of 11 double and 2 rooms with double bed. Each room in this villa contains air conditioning, cable TV, wireless internet, mini bar, optical detector smoke and bathroom with shower and hairdryer. The staff of this accommodation will be at your disposal 24 hours a day. The price of accommodation includes tourist tax and breakfast.
