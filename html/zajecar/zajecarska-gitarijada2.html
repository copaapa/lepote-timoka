Rock festival 'Gitarijada'

Zajecar Guitar Festival is one of the oldest music festivals in this area, which can be said and that it is one of the oldest musical events in the entire Balkans. It was created back in 1966, and its goal was and will remain the encouragement and affirmation of the creativity of young people committed to rock and roll.

It takes place in August, and demo bands from the country and the region compete. The event lasts for three days and the evening is completed by famous singers and bands from the country and abroad. The winners of this competition win a certain cash prize as an incentive to release your album.

The patron of this music festival is the city of Zajecar.
