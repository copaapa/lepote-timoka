Crnorecje in song and dance

The festival "Crnorecje in song and dance" has been held in Boljevac for half a century, and is held every June or July. The goal of this festival is to preserve and revitalize the ethno-cultural heritage in the area of ​​Crna Reka, and now the entire Boljevac municipality. The specificity of this festival is reflected in the intertwining of ethnology and culture of Serbian and Vlach population living along the Black Timok. This event lasts about four days and brings together the largest number of authentic rural folklore groups throughout Timok.

As part of the event, an exhibition of local handicrafts and traditional dishes "Golden Hands of Crnorecje" is organized, which presents old crafts, folk handicrafts, cuisine and souvenirs of the Timok region. They are screened at this festival and customs that originate from Slavic mythology and remember past times.

The main contents of the manifestation "Crnorecje u pesmi i igri" are art colonies, exhibitions, promotion and guest appearances of famous cultural and artistic societies from the country and abroad.

The organizers of this festival are the Tourist Organization of Boljevac, the Municipality of Boljevac and the Ministry of Culture of the Republic of Serbia.
