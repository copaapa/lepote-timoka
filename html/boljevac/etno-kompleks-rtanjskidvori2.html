Ethno Complex Rtanjski Dvori

Located at the foot of the mountain Garvanica, in the village of Ilino, near the mountain Rtanj, there is an ethno complex "Rtanjski dvori". In the village itself, there is Vilino vrelo, from which drinking water springs, from the stone of the mountain Garvanica. This ethno complex consists of four apartments, with a capacity of 2 to 6 people. Each apartment has a bedroom, living room, dining room, kitchen, bathroom, terrace overlooking the Fairy Spring, air conditioning, cable TV. Within this complex there is a secured parking lot. Separate from this complex is the restaurant "Dusanova mehana", where guests of the ethno complex and other visitors are served traditional cuisine, and food is delivered by locals. This complex and the village of Ilino are four kilometers away from the town of Boljevac, and about 7 kilometers from the mountain Rtanj.
