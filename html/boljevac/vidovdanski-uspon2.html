Night ascent to Rtanj on St.Vitus day

Under the auspices of the mountaineering club "Siljak" from Boljevac, every June, during Vidovdan, the republic mountaineering action "Night ascent to Rtanj" is organized. Trail with a total length of 14 kilometers starts from the village Rtanj at an altitude of 610 meters, on the south side of the mountain, and goes along the forest path to the beginning the first serpentine. In about four hours you can reach the Å iljak mountain at 1560 meters above sea level.

The action program looks like this:

  • 00:15 - gathering of action participants and preparations for the ascent,
  • 00:30 - Opening Ceremony,
  • 00.45 - departure to climb the south side,
  • 04:30 - going to the top and watching the sunrise,
  • 05:00 - 08:00 - time provided for staying at the top of the mountain,
  • 08:00 - 11:00 - descent on the south side to the settlement Rtanj,
  • 11:00 - the official closing ceremony of the action begins, the awarding of certificates of appreciation, gold, silver and bronze badges to the participants of the mountaineering action.

All participants are required to provide adequate equipment when climbing Rtanj, due to low temperatures at the very top of the mountain, such as adequate clothing, footwear, spare clothes (raincoat), drinking water, glasses and sunscreen and certified mountaineering and health booklet.

All interested participants can contact the mountaineering club "Siljak" for detailed information and to register for the climb.
