Hotel Ramonda

In the heart of the mountain Rtanj is located a hotel "Ramonda". It was named after the autochthonous flower that adorns the mountain Rtanj and the Boljevac region. This facility is located in the forest itself, separated from the city noise and commotion, surrounded only by forest and the remains of an abandoned coal mine. The hotel opened in 2019. This property offers its guests 21 accommodation units, double rooms and suites. Each room has a bathroom with a shower, an extra sofa bed, cable TV, a terrace and free WiFi. Some rooms are larger and contain more rooms. Within the hotel "Ramonda" there is a small artificial lake made exclusively for hotel guests. In it, water is purified naturally without chemicals, only with the help of special plants. During autumn and winter, guests have access to an indoor pool with panoramic windows. The hotel also offers a sauna and a steam bath with a large hot tub. If you need a massage, you can always hire top masseurs, who can offer you first-class treatments, which would be completely dedicated to your relaxation. If you need physical activity, within the hotel you will find a modern fitness center equipped with exercise belts, dry paddles, bicycles and other modern exercise equipment. The restaurant of the hotel "Ramonda" offers its guests dishes of traditional cuisine, which are served in a unique and modern way, with quality wines, and the hotel also includes in its menu dishes for guests with a special diet. A special offer is made by organic dishes made from the best local ingredients. Hotel "Ramonda" is located on the mountain Rtanj, in the street Rudnička 38, away from Boljevac 6 kilometers.
