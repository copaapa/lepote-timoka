Ethno Hotel Balasevic

Ethno hotel "Rtanj Balasevic" with three stars, is located in the village of Mirovo, eight kilometers from Boljevac. Within this hotel, you can find several complexes: 1. Savior's house; 2. Doctor's house; 3. Bungalows; 4. Hajduk house. Within all these complexes there are double rooms, with bathrooms and showers, TVs and cable network and each room has free WiFi internet. The hotel features an indoor and outdoor pool and a wellness center for its guests. The pool is made with a movable roof, special hydromassage effects and an exotic interior. The state-of-the-art system of automatic water disinfection is installed inside the pool, so that the water is optimal and modernly clean at all times, regardless of the number of bathers in it. Also, the pool contains a hydro-massage system with the help of which the water temperature increases, and allows its guests to be relieved of tension and accumulated stress. The hotel restaurant has a capacity of 250 seats, where it offers the possibility of organizing various types of business lunches, banquets and seminars. The restaurant menu includes traditional local cuisine. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel is located in the village of Mirovo, Magistralni put bb.
