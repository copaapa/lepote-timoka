Villa Lovac

Villa "Lovac" is located in one of the most beautiful weekend resorts on Lake Bor. It is positioned 100 meters from the main road and about 200 meters from the main beach. Also, the nearest store is only a fifteen minute walk away. In addition to accommodation, this villa offers its guests a kitchen and living room. It also has a spacious courtyard that allows for safe car parking during your stay. Accommodation facilities in the villa "Lovac" are available to tourists from July 1 until September 15.
