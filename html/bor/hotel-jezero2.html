Hotel Jezero

Hotel "Jezero" is located on the shores of Lake Bor, 17 kilometers from Bor and belongs to the category of four-star hotels. It consists of modern rooms with a total capacity of 240 beds. The rooms are double, triple and family, and also consists of apartments and lux rooms. All rooms have a modern bathroom, terrace, air conditioning, safe, LCD TV and WiFi internet. The hotel restaurant is spacious, with a capacity of up to 350 seats, and it can organize all kinds of entertainment programs and seminars. Hotel "Jezero" offers for all athletes and recreationists offers many outdoor and indoor facilities intended only for their needs. Next to the hotel there are sports fields for football, basketball, tennis, volleyball and handball. The hotel also has a billiards room, a modern bowling alley, a squash room, a gym, a massage room, a swimming pool, a sauna and a jacuzzi. The hotel also includes various SPA treatments and massages for its guests at an additional cost.
