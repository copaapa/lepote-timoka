Miner's day

The city of Bor celebrates the Miners' Day every sixth of August, which is marked by various exhibitions, sale of various souvenirs, performances for the youngest, and in the evening on the town square are held music concerts of famous performers, with this being omitted this year due to the poor epidemiological situation.

This day is commemorated on August 6, 1903, when the miners of the Senj mine near Despotovac went on strike to fight for the return to work of three fired colleagues, for a better position for the miners and for shortening working hours.

This event is complemented by the awarding of the "Shistek Award" to employees of the mining company ZIJIN in Bor, for the greatest contribution to the development of this company. In the evening, this celebration continues with a series of music concerts and with the sound of a trumpet, and finally ends with fireworks.
