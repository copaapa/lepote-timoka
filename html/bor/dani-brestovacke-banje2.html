Days of Brestovacka spa

The traditional manifestation "Days of Brestovacka Spa" is celebrated during the summer, before the Orthodox holiday of the Transfiguration of Jesus, where he dedicates himself to the mentioned holiday and spa, with an appropriate cultural and artistic program.

The event is still marked by three symbols - the first is the arrival of the Prince, and almost every year in the role of Prince Milos Obrenović is the famous Serbian actor Branko Jerinić, while in the role of Princess Ljubica is mostly actress Katarina Vićentijević. The second symbol is the traditional lighting of the Transfiguration fire, and the third is the accompanying manifestation "Golden Hands", where competitors can participate in three categories: preparing traditional dishes, making souvenirs and making objects using old crafts.

Also, during this event, a competition is held in the preparation of old dishes in the way that once seemed. This competition gathers participants from all over Eastern Serbia, and beyond, where they will compete in culinary magic. There is also a competition in making handicrafts in old crafts.

The Cultural Center of the City of Bor and the Tourist Organization of the City of Bor also organize a rich cultural and artistic program, with the performance of cultural and artistic societies and theater troupes, and the evening is always complemented by the performance of pop personalities from Serbia and the region.
