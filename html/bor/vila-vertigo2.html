Villa Vertigo

Villa Vertigo is located on the Main Beach of Lake Bor, just 100 meters from the beach. This villa has 17 rooms, 12 of which are overlooking the lake. Each room is equipped with bathroom and shower, air conditioning, LCD TV and free WiFi internet. Within the villa there is a restaurant that offers board service for its guests. It also contains parking for its guests.
